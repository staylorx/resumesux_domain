## Job Description

ConstructionPro Builders is seeking an experienced Heavy Equipment Operator to join our team on various construction sites. You'll operate and maintain heavy machinery for excavation, grading, and site preparation projects.

## Responsibilities

- Operate bulldozers, excavators, loaders, and graders safely and efficiently
- Perform site grading and excavation work
- Load and unload materials using heavy equipment
- Maintain equipment and perform basic repairs
- Follow safety protocols and traffic control procedures
- Read blueprints and understand project specifications
- Communicate with site supervisors and team members

## Requirements

- 3+ years of experience operating heavy equipment
- Valid commercial driver's license (CDL) preferred
- OSHA safety certifications
- Ability to work outdoors in various weather conditions
- Strong mechanical aptitude and problem-solving skills
- Physical fitness and ability to lift heavy objects
- High school diploma or equivalent

## Nice to Have

- Experience with GPS-guided equipment
- Welding and fabrication skills
- First aid/CPR certification
- Experience in pipeline or mining operations

## Benefits

- Competitive hourly wage with overtime opportunities
- Health insurance and retirement plan
- Paid time off and holidays
- Safety bonuses and performance incentives
- On-the-job training and certification support
- Stable work with established company
