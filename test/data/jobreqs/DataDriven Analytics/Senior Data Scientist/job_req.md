## Job Description

DataDriven Analytics is looking for a Senior Data Scientist to join our growing team. You'll work on advanced analytics projects, develop machine learning models, and drive data-driven insights for our clients across various industries.

## Responsibilities

- Develop and deploy machine learning models for predictive analytics
- Analyze large datasets to extract actionable business insights
- Collaborate with engineering teams to integrate ML solutions into production
- Design and implement A/B tests and experimental frameworks
- Create data visualizations and reports for stakeholders
- Mentor junior data scientists and contribute to best practices

## Requirements

- PhD or Master's in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or related field
- 4+ years of experience in data science and machine learning
- Proficiency in Python, R, and SQL
- Experience with ML frameworks (scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch)
- Strong statistical analysis and modeling skills
- Experience with big data tools (Spark, Hadoop) preferred

## Nice to Have

- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure)
- Knowledge of deep learning and NLP techniques
- Publications in data science conferences or journals
- Experience with MLOps and model deployment

- Competitive salary with performance bonuses
- Comprehensive health and retirement benefits
- Flexible work arrangements and remote options
- Conference attendance and professional development
- Cutting-edge projects with real impact
- Collaborative and innovative work environment
